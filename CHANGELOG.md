## [0.1.0] - 2023-11-16

### Added

- `--rerun-fresh` parameter.
- `--rerun-iter` parameter.
- Support for [pytest-store].
- Improve _README_.

### Changed

- `--rerun-for` parameter to `--rerun-time`.
- Replace _pandas_ dependency with _dateparser_.
